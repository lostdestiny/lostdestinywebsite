About Us

We are Lost Destiny, a collective of musicians driven by our passion for creating electrifying rock music. Each of us brings our unique sound and energy to the stage, blending together to form a dynamic and unforgettable musical experience.

  • Nuh Lujan – Lead Guitarist
    With his razor-sharp riffs and powerful solos, Nuh is the backbone of our sound, crafting melodies that captivate and energize audiences every time we play.
  • Alyson Rodriguez – Rhythm Guitarist
    Alyson brings the pulse and groove to the band, laying down the perfect rhythm that keeps us grounded and driving forward, creating a rock-solid foundation for our music.
  • Sebastian Almeida – Bass Guitarist
    The heartbeat of our band, Sebastian’s bass lines are deep, smooth, and unmistakable, giving our tracks their groove and soul.
  • Zachary Sanchez – Drummer & Co-Singer
    Zachary’s beats are the force that propels us forward. As our drummer and co-singer, he gives the band its rhythm and voice, keeping the tempo tight while adding his unique vocal flair.
  • Marisol Perez – Lead Singer
    Marisol’s vocals are raw, powerful, and full of emotion. Her stage presence is magnetic, drawing you in with every note and making you feel every word she sings.

Together, we are a band that thrives on energy, passion, and an unwavering commitment to bringing you the best rock music. Whether we're on stage or in the studio, we pour our hearts into every song, every note, and every performance. Stay tuned, because this is just the beginning.
